About TechForDad

We help adult children and caregivers find the right tech to keep aging parents safe, connected, and independent.

Our Mission

TechForDad was built out of a personal frustration: when our own parents started needing help with technology and safety devices, we found the internet full of sponsored rankings and outdated reviews that didn't reflect real-world experience.

We built TechForDad because the information we needed when helping our own parents didn't exist — or was buried under sponsored rankings and outdated specs. Every recommendation on this site is the result of deep, structured research rather than a quick product roundup.

How We Research

Every product we recommend goes through the same evaluation process before we publish:

  • Manufacturer spec analysis — we read every spec sheet, contract term, and fine-print detail so you don't have to
  • Verified review analysis — we screen hundreds of verified buyer reviews on Amazon, ConsumerAffairs, and the BBB to identify real patterns in what seniors and caregivers actually experience
  • Customer service evaluation — we contact each company's support line to assess response times, hold times, and the quality of guidance given
  • Expert input — recommendations are informed by published guidance from occupational therapists, audiologists, and geriatric care specialists
  • Quarterly re-evaluation — we revisit every recommendation regularly; pricing, contracts, and product quality change, and our rankings change with them

Our Promise to You

We earn affiliate commissions when you buy through our links — but this never changes our rankings. Products earn their position through research, not payment. If a product has consistently poor reviews or unfair contract terms, we say so plainly regardless of commission rates.

We will never recommend something we wouldn't direct our own family to buy.

Affiliate Disclosure

This is how we keep the site free. Our editorial decisions are completely independent of our affiliate relationships.
