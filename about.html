About SeniorTechGuide

We help adult children and caregivers find the right tech to keep aging parents safe, connected, and independent.

Our Mission

SeniorTechGuide was built out of a personal frustration: when our own parents started needing help with technology and safety devices, we found the internet full of sponsored rankings and outdated reviews that didn't reflect real-world experience.

We started testing products ourselves — calling monitoring centers at 2am, dropping devices in the kitchen, handing gadgets to real seniors aged 68–88 and watching what happened. What you read on this site is the result of that testing.

How We Test

Every product we recommend has been through our standard evaluation process:

  • Hands-on testing — we use each device ourselves for at least two weeks
  • Senior user testing — we give devices to real seniors and observe first-hand
  • Customer service testing — we call each company's support line multiple times
  • Long-term follow-up — we revisit recommendations monthly as products change

Our Promise to You

We accept affiliate commissions from some companies when you buy through our links — but this never changes our rankings. Products earn their spot on our lists through testing, not payment. If a product fails our tests, we say so plainly.

We will never recommend something we wouldn't put in our own parents' home.

Affiliate Disclosure

SeniorTechGuide participates in the Amazon Associates Program and other affiliate programs. When you click links on our site and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. This is how we fund our testing and keep the site free for everyone.

Our editorial decisions are completely independent of our affiliate relationships.
