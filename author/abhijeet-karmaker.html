About Abhijeit

Abhijeit Karmaker founded TechForDad after years of helping his father navigate the rapidly changing world of consumer technology. What started as a personal mission — finding the right medical alert system, simplifying the family smartphone, setting up fall detection — turned into a full testing operation after Abhijeit realized how poorly served caregivers and seniors were by existing review sites.

Most senior tech reviews online focus on specs alone and don't account for the real-world experience of an 80-year-old with limited dexterity, reduced hearing, or no background with touchscreen interfaces. Every product on TechForDad is evaluated through that lens — cross-referencing manufacturer specs with what verified senior users actually report in their own words.

Background & Credentials

  • Technology background — Abhijeit has worked in the technology sector for over a decade, giving him the technical foundation to cut through marketing claims and evaluate products on their actual engineering merits.
  • Caregiver experience — As the primary technology point-of-contact for his aging father, Abhijeit has personally researched, purchased, and set up dozens of senior tech products in a real home environment.
  • Healthcare-informed research — Product evaluations on TechForDad are informed by published guidance from occupational therapists, audiologists, and geriatric care specialists on usability considerations for seniors with mobility, cognitive, or sensory limitations.
  • Ongoing testing — Abhijeit revisits every product recommendation at least quarterly to account for firmware updates, pricing changes, and new competitors entering the market.

How TechForDad Reviews Work

Every review on this site follows a structured research process before anything is published:

  1. Specification deep-dive — Abhijeit reads every spec sheet, contract term, and manufacturer claim in detail before forming any opinion on a product.
  2. Verified review analysis — Hundreds of verified buyer reviews on Amazon, ConsumerAffairs, and the BBB are screened to identify real patterns in what seniors and caregivers actually experience — positive and negative.
  3. Customer service evaluation — Each company's support line is contacted to assess responsiveness, hold times, and the quality of guidance provided to potential customers.
  4. Expert and caregiver input — Recommendations are informed by published guidance from occupational therapists, audiologists, and geriatric care specialists, as well as direct input from the caregiver community.
  5. Quarterly re-evaluation — Every recommendation is revisited regularly. Pricing changes, contracts change, and product quality changes — rankings are updated accordingly.

Editorial Independence

TechForDad earns revenue through affiliate commissions when you purchase products through links on this site. This never influences which products are ranked, recommended, or covered. Products earn their position through research quality, not payment. Abhijeit has removed products from top positions despite higher affiliate commissions when research showed consistently poor real-world experiences reported by users.
