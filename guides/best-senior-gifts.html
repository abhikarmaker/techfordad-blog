Best Tech Gifts for Seniors

Thoughtful gift ideas that are actually easy for seniors to use and enjoy.

The Best Gift Is One They'll Actually Use

The best tech gift for a senior isn't always the newest gadget — it's one that's simple enough to use without a tutorial, and that solves a real problem like staying in touch with family, getting around safely, or enjoying photos and shows.

What to Look For

• A simple tablet pre-loaded with family photos and a video-calling app — see our Tablets for Seniors guide.
• A senior-friendly cell phone with large buttons and an emergency button — see our Cell Phones for Seniors guide.
• A digital picture frame that family members can update remotely with new photos.
• A medical alert system — a gift that gives both safety and independence (and peace of mind for the whole family).
• A large-button universal remote for seniors who find modern TV remotes overwhelming.
